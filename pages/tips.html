How to Report Bugs


Bugs is a part of plugin development. I tries my best to reduce bugs but they can always come in unexpected ways. If you see a bug report, always remember to press FN+F8 to open the console and take a screenshot of the console page. Otherwise, nobody would know what's going wrong.
