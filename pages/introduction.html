Introduction


SRPG Engine MV, also known as SRPG Converter MV or SRPGコンバータＭＶ, is a series of plugins that convert RPG Maker MV to a turn-based strategic role-playing game engine. This project was initiated by Gakuto Mikagami, the author of SRPG_Core. Later, several developers including me contributed to this project by making extensions. This site is my portfolio on the SRPG Converter MV project.

My plugins greatly improve the battle system and UI. Using my plugin, you can build SRPG games with interesting features you might see in other famous SRPG games like Fire Emblem. My plugins realized battle preparation, advanced AI, AoE battle system, event interactions, aura skills, summoning skills, and more. They also allow you to visualize important information such as battler status, attack range, and terrain effects.

The Discussion Forum is the largest SRPG engine community. You can find most information about the engine and other great plugins here. There are some other great resources that I found and put together, click 'Resources' on the navigation bar to view them.
