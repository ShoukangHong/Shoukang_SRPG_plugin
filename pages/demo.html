Shoukang SRPG Demo

Jan 24 2022    v1.03    Download

This demo project illustrates the usage and effects of my plugins, it also includes a comprehensive turn-based strategic game to demonstrate the power of all plugins combined. All the resources in the demo are free to use in RPG Maker projects. All the plugins used are under MIT license.

This demo project inherits all the features in SRPG MV demo 1.32Q. If you haven't already, I highly recommend trying the 1.32Q demo too!
